Attractions

Mouth

Entrance Booth in the shape of a mouth (entrance tickets are in the shape of food - adult tickets are hamburgers and kid tickets are fries; tickets will be “stamped” with a bite mark).

The Esophagus Drop

A drop-like ride in the shape of the esophagus; starts by going up but “bounces” its way down in order to demonstrate peristalsis in the esophagus

Acid Trip

A roller coaster in the shape of the stomach and small intestine; the cart starts off yellow, and begins as a calm ride through the “acid” of the stomach and turns green until it eventually goes up and turns into a swirly roller coaster representing the small intestine; the roller coaster will have a tunnel with dangling microvilli, and as riders will go through it, their cart will finally turn brown, showing that the nutrients have been absorbed

Kiddie Colon

A kiddie roller coaster water ride in the shape of a large intestine.

Bile Station

Make Your Own Bile Here and Keep It Like The Gallbladder! Booth will be in the shape of the liver; kids can either keep their “bile” or throw it at a board to “break down” excess fat and get a bigger prize.

Pancreas Arcade

Full of pancreas-themed games: A whack-a-mole type game that mimics the pancreas in function and will have three different versions - Whack A Fat, Whack A Carb, and Whack A Protein; each of the “hammers” in the game will be a different enzyme for their respective nutrients, Insulin-Glucagon Balance game, where two players will be on balance-sensitive pads where they will try to balance out the level of insulin and glucagon by jumping or running in place.

Food Court

It stands as the rectum as it represents the “holding area” until visitors are “released." Here there will be many delicious degestive system themed foods to eat. Such foods include Intestinal Cake (funnel cake), Rotten Candy (food that's broken down), Sausage Party (intestine: hot dogs), and more!

Exit Anus

Exit in the shape of the anus (with a toilet Hollywood sign saying “Thanks for Digesting!”)
