DigestiveLand

About Us

Disgestive Land's mission is to teach kids about the structure and function of the digestive system in a fun and engaging way. Everything in our park is named based off the digestive system from the Acid Trip to the Intestinal Cake, which you can purchase at the food court. Every attraction is meant to demonstrate how specific organs work to make sure that the digestive system functions properly. Contact us to purchase tickets beforehand or buy them at the entrance. Digestive Land is open to anyone and everyone. Come get digested today!
